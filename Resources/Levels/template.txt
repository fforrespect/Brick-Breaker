## a space and '.' mean the same thing (nothing)
## a brick is formatted as: "{level_number}{powerup}"
## where:
##      level_number is a number 0-7, 0 making the brick unbreakable
##      powerup is a character indicating which powerup goes in that brick
## powerups:
##      l : extra Life
##      m : Multi ball (splits the ball into 4)
##      c : Catch (allows you to catch the ball)
##      g : Gun (gives you a gun)
##      e : long paddle (Extends the paddle width)
